# Information Collection Template

For each work experience, collect the following. Ask in focused batches, not all at once.

---

## Basic Info
- Company name (in original language + English if applicable)
- Job title (original + English)
- Date range
- Team size and your role: IC / Tech Lead / Manager / Coordinator

## Core Achievements (3-5 per role)
- What did you do? (specific action)
- What technology did you use? (only confirmed ones)
- What was the result? (exact number if you have it, estimate if approximate, description if unsure)
- What was the scope of impact? (users, repos, teams, revenue)

## Leadership Signals
- How many people did you lead/mentor? At what level?
- What technical decisions did you own?
- What processes or standards did you establish?

## Reliability Signals
- What monitoring/observability tools did you use?
- Did you handle production incidents? What was your role?
- What risk-reduction measures did you implement?
